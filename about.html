Who We Are

918.software is Devin Venable — an independent software consultant based in Tulsa, Oklahoma. I work directly with operations teams in small and mid-sized businesses to remove the friction from internal workflows and systems.

Problems I Solve

I work with operations teams who are stuck with:

  • Spreadsheets that became systems — brittle, error-prone, impossible to hand off
  • Manual processes that eat time — copying data between tools, chasing updates, fixing mistakes
  • Multiple tools that almost work together — but require constant workarounds
  • Internal tools people rely on but dislike — clunky, outdated, or built by someone who left
  • Reporting that takes hours — pulling from multiple sources, reformatting, double-checking

These aren't glamorous problems. But they compound over time, and they're exactly the kind of thing I fix.
