Practical AI Skills

Your team is probably already experimenting with AI tools. The question is whether they're using them well — or wasting time on bad outputs and risky shortcuts.

I run training sessions focused on your actual work: the emails you write, the reports you generate, the questions you answer. Not theory. Not hype. Just practical skills your team can use the next day.

Team Workshops

Half-day or full-day sessions with your team. We work through your actual tasks and build skills together.

One-on-One Coaching

Individual sessions for managers or specialists who need focused attention on their specific use cases.

Risk & Guardrails

AI makes mistakes. I teach your team how to catch them — and when not to use AI at all.

Custom Prompt Libraries

I build a set of tested prompts for your recurring tasks — so your team isn't starting from scratch every time.

What Your Team Will Learn

  • When to use AI (and when not to): Not everything needs AI. I'll help your team recognize the difference.
  • Writing prompts that work: Clear instructions that get consistent, useful results — not garbage.
  • Catching mistakes: AI hallucinates. Your team needs to know what to double-check.
  • Privacy and risk: What's safe to put into AI tools? What isn't? Clear guidelines for your business.
  • Building on results: How to iterate and refine outputs instead of starting over.
  • Your actual workflows: We work through real examples from your business, not hypotheticals.
